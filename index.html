Our MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) event is centered around **yoga** in the serene backdrop of the Annapurna Mountains. The event's purpose is twofold: to showcase the breathtaking natural beauty and rich biodiversity of Nepal, and to provide a rejuvenating escape from the stresses of modern life. By blending the tranquility of yoga with the majestic surroundings, we aim to offer participants a unique opportunity to reconnect with nature, promote wellness, and embrace peace amidst the hustle of today's fast-paced era.
